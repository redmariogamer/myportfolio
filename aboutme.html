Welcome to my website!

Daniel Francavilla is an aspiring game designer and artist motivated to bring his work to the table. Born and raised in Fremont, California, he is currently finishing his studies at Cal State East Bay to pursue a degree in Bachelor of Fine Arts with a concentration of Interaction and Game Design.

As someone who has fascination with video games and willing to get invovled with the current trends of the community, he is committed to strive to present entertaining and meaningful content for people of all ages.

He is currently working towards his senior year of college while seeking to gain professional experience through the entertainment industry such as Nintendo, Sony, Roblox, Nickelodeon, Dreamworks, or Pixar.
