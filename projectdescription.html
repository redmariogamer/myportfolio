Echoes of Four Realm

An Unreal Engine group project for class where it focuses on the story with a young boy named Milo who lives in a cottage house on a hill with his grandmother. One day, his grandmother has caught an unknown illness which had threathened his mother's life. Mylo the player you will be playing will be in charge of collecting all of the ingredients and combine them together can heal any known and unknown illness known to humanity. His only living family member is sick and the only way to stop this from happening is to find the magical ingrediant in all four realm that sometihng that his father wasn't able to accomplish.
