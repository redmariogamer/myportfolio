European Voyage: Legends of the Hidden Relics (on haitus)

A solo game to learn about life around Europe that gives Assassin Creed's vibes. Created in Summer 2025 with inspiration of the Dreamworks Movie, Mr. Peabody and Sherman.

A co-op 1–4 player adventure set in a 3D European-inspired world. Players are guided by a mysterious professor who advises them to go on a race to recover ancient relics before an evil sorcerer erases the relics and the memory itself. Exploration, decision-making, and discovery are the neccessary factors to drive the quest.
